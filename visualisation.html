Interactive Visualizations

Explore NDVI and Soil Moisture data through Google Earth Engine

NDVI Mapping

Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) shows vegetation health across India. Green areas indicate healthy vegetation, while brown/red areas show vegetation stress.

Soil Moisture Mapping

Soil moisture content in the root zone (0-100cm depth). Blue indicates high moisture content, while red/orange shows dry conditions.

How to Use These Visualizations

Layer Selection

Use the dropdown menu to switch between NDVI, Soil Moisture, and SPI visualizations to compare different drought indicators.

Time Control

Select different months to observe seasonal patterns and drought evolution over the 2019-2023 period.

Interactive Map

Click on specific regions within the map to view detailed statistics and historical trends for that area.

Data Export

Download region-specific data for further analysis or integration with your own systems.
